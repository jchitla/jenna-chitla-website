About Me

I'm an Implementation Specialist who helps enterprise clients bring SaaS products to life, and make them thrive post-launch! I love making technology click for people. Every implementation feels like a puzzle to me, and the fun part is figuring out how all the pieces fit together for each client.

Over the years, I've led end-to-end SaaS implementations across industries, from restaurant technology at Olo to digital signage and employee communications at Poppulo. My experience spans everything from client discovery and requirements gathering to configuration, QA testing, and training. I work cross-functionally with product, engineering, and customer success teams to ensure every rollout is smooth and scalable.
